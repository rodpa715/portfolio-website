Who am I?

I'm Patrick Rodrigues, born and raised by Portuguese parents in the little Grand-Duchy Of Luxembourg.

I like to think of myself as a young curiosity driven person. What I most seek in life is the wisdom and knowledge that can be acquired by surmounting the obstacles that lie ahead of us.

I'm currently learning program so I that can become a professional Full Stack Web Developper and I've learned a lot about programming so far, but for me the biggest lesson is that motivation is the bi-product of just doing, of following through.

Motivation is not found, it is created.

My Vision

My family's descendants played a big role in the explorations of the indian ocean, their vision of seeing beyond the horizon made them go down in history.
I'd like to follow in their footsteps by bringing in innovative technologies and solutions into this world. A world desperate for creative minded people who look at problems from the perspective of a solution.

This body of ours is the single well designed piece of hardware and software on earth and my main goal in this life is to discover, decipher and crack the code to the human body. My ultimate goal is to enable people to reach their true potential,y by unlocking parts and tapping into regions that were previously untapped.
